Drill Practice — 5 Prompts (Task 1 Independent)

Example Model Answer (Prompt 1)

I agree that students should be required to take physical education classes in college. First, regular exercise is essential for maintaining both physical and mental health, especially during stressful academic periods. For example, when I was in university, the students who exercised regularly were more focused and performed better in their classes. Moreover, physical education provides a necessary break from sitting in classrooms all day, which helps students stay energized and productive.

Structure Note

Task 1 requires: (1) clear stance in first sentence, (2) one reason with a connector, (3) concrete example. This week, focus on maintaining coherence — do NOT let the idea drift. Use connectors to bridge your thoughts.
