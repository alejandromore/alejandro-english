TOEFL Reading — Practice Section

Based on The Official Guide to the TOEFL iBT Test 2021

📖 Reading Strategies ▼
  • Skim the passage first for the main idea — read the first and last sentences of each paragraph.
  • Read each question carefully — identify what type of question it is before looking at the options.
  • Use context clues for vocabulary — look at surrounding sentences to determine word meaning.
  • Look for transition words (however, therefore, although) — they signal relationships between ideas.
  • Manage your time: approximately 20 minutes per passage set.
